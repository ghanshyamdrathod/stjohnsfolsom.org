Greeters Ministry

Mission Statement

Our mission is to encourage and enrich the lives of each person entering the doors of St. John the Baptist Church by welcoming and supporting them in a positive, friendly and spiritual manner.

Purpose

The purpose of this ministry is to provide an enthusiastic, positive, spiritual and friendly welcome to each person entering St. John's Church before every worship service.

Activities

The activities of this ministry shall therefore be designed to be supportive and sensitive to the needs of the church and the congregation by assisting the priests, deacons, ushers, and other ministries as needed. Opportunities shall be provided for the greeters to assist before and after each Mass as needed.

Services

The services rendered by the Greeters Ministry shall be to:

  • welcome each person entering for worship at St. John's in a friendly, spiritual manner
  • provide assistance, information and direction to church facilities and services
  • educate members of the ministry on maintaining positive, spiritual attitudes
  • support the mission and the goals of the church
