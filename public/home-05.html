Adult Faith Formation

Welcome to St. John the Baptist Catholic Church!

Mission

Faith formation is a primary goal here at St. John the Baptist Parish. We are a faith community committed to learning and serving together with Christ. We provide extensive opportunities to learn more about our Catholic faith and serve God and our community.

Faith formation is a life-long journey. Through evangelization and catechesis, the church works to bring the initial conversion, and to lead the believer to a more mature faith and a life in Christ. In journey, all are welcome.

Our Goal

The various faith formation ministries at St. John the Baptist parish are designed to help Catholics become fully formed and transformed in their Catholic faith.
