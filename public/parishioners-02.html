Parent Communications

Parent communication is important at St. John the Evangelist School. The faculty strives to keep families informed of current events and activities in the classrooms.

Families receive a weekly Newsletter from the school office. Weekly envelopes which contain the Newsletter plus other important information about upcoming events and activities are sent home with students every Thursday throughout the school year.
