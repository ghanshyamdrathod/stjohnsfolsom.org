Faith Formation

Welcome to St. John the Baptist's Faith Formation Ministry!

Faith formation is a life-long journey. Through evangelization and catechesis, the Church works to bring about initial conversion, and to lead the believer to a more mature faith and a life in Christ. In this journey, all are welcome.

Please visit our Faith Formation pages. We offer faith programs and activities for children through adults.
