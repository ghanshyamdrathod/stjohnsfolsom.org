Newcomer Welcome

Welcome to St. John the Baptist Catholic Church!

Our parish has been in Folsom for over 150 years and is committed to being an inclusive and welcoming family. We respond through active ministries as a reflection of God’s love and witness to the Gospel in prayer, word, and deed.

We look forward to meeting you and we invite you to join us for Mass. The Mass schedule at St. John’s is set to accommodate the needs of you and your families. Whether you are searching for a faith community, just starting out on your faith journey, or coming home to the Catholic Church, you will find St. John the Baptist Catholic Church a safe and welcoming place to further your faith and allow you to grow closer to God!

There are many ministries at St. John’s that serve the community and we are blessed with the many volunteers that support them. On our website, you will find information about these ministries and the activities in the parish for children, students, and adults. Our liturgical, spiritual and social gatherings, ministries, and parish events reflect the love of our parish members and will help you grow in your faith and relationship with God. I encourage you to join us, share your talents and gifts, and become a part of this faith community.

Yours in Christ,

Fr. Rey Bersabal
Pastor
